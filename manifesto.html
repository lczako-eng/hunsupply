Brand Bible

Tools for Men Who Roam.

Brand Essence

Core Idea

Hun Supply is a nomadic, earth-born, ultra-masculine grooming house for men who refuse to lose.

We do not sell soap. We sell philosophy, identity, and discipline.

  • Men who roam
  • Men who build
  • Men who carry their own weight
  • Men who aren’t afraid of pain
  • Men who live by their own rules

Brand Philosophy

Voice

Writing Rules

  • Minimal. Stoic. Direct. Earthy.
  • No jokes. No quirk. No fluff.
  • Short. Clipped. Evocative.
  • Never say: “bro”, “alpha”, “brotherhood”, “grit & grind”, “artisan”, “handmade soap”.
  • Say instead: Tools. Work. Weathered. Earned. Used leather. Old soil.

Product

The Rule

Every product must feel like a tool a horseman would carry 1,000 years ago — if he lived today.

The Line

  • Bar One — Earth Clay (wet soil after storm)
  • Bar Two — Leather Smoke (raw hide, cold oak fire, ash, salt)
  • Bar Three — Pine Air (wild pine resin, fir, altitude air)
  • Steppe Oil — Beard & Body (one bottle: hair, beard, skin)
  • Forge Balm — Heavy clay balm (matte finish, zero shine)

Retail

Core Position

  • Wholesale-only.
  • No direct-to-consumer.
  • No discounting.
  • Retailers must apply. Most will be declined.

Non-Negotiables

  • Earth and leather must always be present
  • Tone remains stoic and disciplined
  • No bright colors
  • No trend chasing
  • Every product must feel like a tool
  • Packaging must look traveled
  • Masculinity is never apologized for
